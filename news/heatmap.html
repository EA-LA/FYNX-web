How to use heatmaps

Find leadership fast Heatmaps help you spot which sectors, coins, or currencies are strongest and weakest right now.
Use it as context Heatmaps help with filtering and direction, but entries should still come from structure and confirmation.
Compare markets Switch between stocks, crypto, forex, and ETFs to understand broader cross-market rotation.

Best use cases

Stocks Find hot sectors, weak sectors, and broad market breadth quickly.
Crypto See where momentum is concentrated and where money is leaving.
Forex Read broad currency strength before choosing which pair to focus on.
ETF Track themes, macro baskets, and broader asset allocations.

Reminder

Do not force trades from color alone Green does not always mean buy and red does not always mean sell. Heatmaps are for context, not blind execution.
