Session liquidity and market behavior.

Use this page to explain where liquidity usually enters the market and when sweeps or expansions are most likely.

Asia Session

Often builds early range and internal liquidity. Good for setting up later London sweeps.

London Session

Usually strongest FX liquidity. Frequent breakouts, reversals, and stop runs around prior session highs/lows.

New York Session

Continuation or reversal depending on London move, US data, and dollar-related flows.
