Pattern recognition reference for traders.

This page gives your users a real pattern reference area they can use while trading. Later you can add scanner logic or screenshots.

Head and Shoulders

Reversal structure. Watch neckline break, retest, and declining momentum into the right shoulder.

Inverse Head and Shoulders

Bullish reversal. Best when formed after an extended downtrend with breakout confirmation.

Double Top

Price fails twice near resistance. Confirmation usually comes from neckline breakdown.

Double Bottom

Price rejects lows twice. Confirmation comes on reclaim of neckline or resistance pivot.

Ascending Triangle

Flat resistance with rising lows. Often continuation when pressure builds upward.

Descending Triangle

Flat support with lower highs. Often continuation when selling pressure increases.

Flag / Pennant

Short consolidation after impulsive move. Useful for continuation entries in strong trends.

Wedge

Compression structure. Direction depends on breakout and higher timeframe context.

Range / Box

Useful for liquidity sweeps, support-resistance trading, and breakout strategies.
