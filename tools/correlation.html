How to use it

Correlation guide

+0.70 to +1.00
Strong positive. They tend to move in the same direction.
+0.30 to +0.69
Moderate positive. Some alignment, but not perfect.
-0.29 to +0.29
Weak or neutral. Little useful directional relationship.
-0.30 to -0.69
Moderate negative. They often move opposite.
-0.70 to -1.00
Strong negative. Strong inverse relationship.

Common examples

EURUSD / GBPUSD Often positive
XAUUSD / DXY Often negative
AUDUSD / NZDUSD Often positive
USDJPY / DXY Context dependent

Trading warning

Do not double your risk just because two pairs look different. If they are highly correlated, your exposure may be almost the same trade.
Correlation changes over time. Recheck during major macro shifts, news cycles, and regime changes.
