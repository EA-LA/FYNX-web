Impact guide

High impact
Can move gold, USD pairs, indices, and yields fast. Reduce risk or wait for confirmation.
Medium impact
Still important for intraday volatility, especially during London and New York.
Low impact
Usually less violent, but still useful for context and sentiment.

What matters most

USD / Gold: CPI, Core CPI, NFP, FOMC, rates, Powell, PCE, unemployment.
EUR pairs: ECB rates, CPI, PMIs, GDP, Lagarde comments.
GBP pairs: BOE rates, CPI, GDP, jobs data, Bailey comments.
JPY pairs: BOJ, inflation, intervention risk, yield headlines.

Execution rule
