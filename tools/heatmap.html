How to read it

  • Green rows show instruments attracting momentum.
  • Red cells show weakness or relative underperformance.
  • Use the strongest vs weakest idea to build cleaner watchlists.
  • Check alignment with your market structure before entering.

Trading use

  • For forex, compare strength across majors before picking a pair.
  • For commodities and crypto, use it to see sector-wide pressure.
  • Do not treat heat alone as an entry signal. It is a context tool.
  • Best use: bias, ranking, and avoiding weak setups.
