Hawkish

Bullish currency

Higher-for-longer rates or upside inflation risks can strengthen the currency and pressure risk assets.

Dovish

Weaker currency

Rate cuts, softer guidance, or recession concerns can weaken the currency and support risk assets.

Neutral

Wait for data
