Trading checklist

1. Price the expectation first

The move usually comes from the surprise versus what markets already priced in.

2. Watch correlated assets

Yields, DXY, gold, equity indices, and bond futures help confirm the real reaction.

3. Focus on guidance, not only the rate

Forward guidance and press-conference tone can matter more than the headline decision.
